What is LogerChain?

LogerChain is a decentralized logistics protocol built on Solana blockchain. It enables on-chain logistics operations, creating a trustless infrastructure for the global supply chain.

While LogerClaw provides the AI intelligence layer for route optimization, LogerChain will provide the infrastructure layer for on-chain logistics operations, settlements, and governance.
